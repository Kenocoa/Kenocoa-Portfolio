Refined Sketch

Includes simple lighting over a cleaned monochrome sketch, a transparent version of the sketch layer can also be provided.

For poses I recommend including props, clothing, hand signs or strong perspective!

Scenes are highly flexible and can include vehicles, interiors, exteriors or landscapes along with the character.

Refined sketches can be made fully rendered at a later date at a reduced cost ie. an $80 fully rendered bust may be commissioned by paying an extra $50 on a $30 refined bust sketch.
